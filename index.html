Welcome to LinguaBridge!

Our mission is to connect languages effortlessly through voice. With our innovative Voice Translator, you can speak into the microphone and see your speech translated into your preferred language in real-time.

How it works:

  1. Speak and Translate:

    • Simply click on the microphone button to start the speech recognition process.
    • Speak clearly into the microphone.
    • Your spoken words will appear as text in the "Your speech will appear here..." section.

  2. Select Your Language:

    • Choose your target language from the drop-down list.
    • Over 80 languages are available, including Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Arabic, and many more.

  3. See the Translation:

    • The translated text will appear in the "Translation will appear here..." section.
    • Experience instant and accurate translations powered by cutting-edge technology.

Features

Easy to Use: A user-friendly interface designed for effortless interaction

Real-time Translation: Fast and reliable speech-to-text and text-to-translation services

Modern Design: A sleek, modern layout that is visually appealing and easy to navigate

Wide Language Support: Translate your speech into numerous languages, accommodating diverse needs

Technologies Used:

  • Speech Recognition: Utilizes the webkitSpeechRecognition API for real-time speech recognition.
  • Translation API: Integrates with the MyMemory Translated API for accurate translations.
  • Responsive Design: Optimized for both desktop and mobile use.

Get Started:

  • Click here to navigate the top.
  • Speak into the microphone by clicking the 'Blue Mic' button.
  • Select your preferred target language from the drop-down menu.
  • View your translated text instantly.
